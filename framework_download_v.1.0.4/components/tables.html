Tables

Tables

Tables are a great way of displaying data in tabular form. Timber only supports the most basic form of tables.

Employee Date of Birth Job Title Hobby
John Jun 21, 1975 Engineer Hiking
Ally Aug 02, 1980 Doctor Biking
Frank Fev 03, 1986 Teacher Swimming
Jane Nov 25, 1970 Lawyer Parachuting
HTML
<table class="table">
 <thead>
  </tr>
   <th>Column 1 Title</th>
   <th>Column 2 Title</th>
  </tr>
 <thead>
 <tbody>
  <tr>
   <th>Column 1 Content</td>
   <td>Column 1 content</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>Column 2 Content</td>
   <td>Column 2 Content</td>
  </tr>
 </tbody>
</table>

Addtional Styles

Tables can be styled further with rounded corners and altered in size. To add rounded corners simply add the .rounded class to the table tag. To alter size simply add .small, .medium, .large or .xlarge classes to the table tag.

Employee Date of Birth Job Title Hobby
John Jun 21, 1975 Engineer Hiking
Ally Aug 02, 1980 Doctor Biking
Frank Fev 03, 1986 Teacher Swimming
Jane Nov 25, 1970 Lawyer Parachuting
HTML
<table class="table rounded large">
 <thead class="bkg-blue-light color-white">
  </tr>
   <th>Column 1 Title</th>
   <th>Column 2 Title</th>
  </tr>
 <thead>
 <tbody>
  <tr>
   <th>Column 1 Content</td>
   <td>Column 1 content</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>Column 2 Content</td>
   <td>Column 2 Content</td>
  </tr>
 </tbody>
</table>
